Banana Cake

Prep Time: 30 mins    Cook Time: 30 mins

Total Estimated Time: 1 hr

Yield: 2-8 inch round layers (12 servings)

Descriprtion

Enjoy a delicious and moist banana cake, packed with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas. This easy-to-make cake is perfect for any occasion, offering a tender crumb and a delightful banana flavour. Serve it plain or with your favourite frosting for a treat everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups (300g) All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Baking Soda
  • 1 pinch (0.5g) Salt
  • 1 cup (200g) White Sugar
  • 3/4 cup (150g) Light Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup (113g) Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 4 Mashed Ripe Bananas (480g)
  • 2/3 cups (160g) Milk
  • 1/2 cup (60g) Chopped Walnuts (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉ (175℃). Grease and flour 2-8 inch round pans.

  2. Whisk together lour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl; set aside

  3. Cream white sugar, brown sugar, and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Mix in mashed bananas. Add flour mixture alternately with milk, mixing well after each addition. Stir in choped walnuts. Put batter into the prepared pans.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cake springs back when gently pressed, about 30 mins.

  5. Remove cake layers from the oven and place them on a damp tea towel to cool.

Enjoy your delicious banana cake!
