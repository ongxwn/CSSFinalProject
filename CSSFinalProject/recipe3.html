Chocolate Tart

Prep Time: 20 mins    Cook Time: 20 mins

Total Estimated Time: 1 hr 10 mins

Yield: 9 tarts

Description

Indulge in these rich and creamy chocolate tarts with a buttery crust. Perfect for dessert, these tarts are a chocolate lover's dream come true. Easy to make and delightful to taste, they're sure to impress at any gathering.

Ingredients

Tart Shells

  • 1/3 cup (75g) Unsalted Butter, Softened
  • 2 tablespoons Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanila Extract
  • 1 cup (120g) All-Purpose Flour

Filling

  • 4 oz (113g) Semi-sweet Chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup (120g) Heavy Cream

Other:

  • Some Sea Salt Flakes

Instructions

Tart Shells

  1. Add butter and sugar into a bowl, and mix well using a spatula. Then add in yolk, and vanila extract and continue mixing. Finally, add in all-purpose flour and bring everything together into a dough. Place and wrap it in a cling film, flattening it to a more or less 1-inch thickness. Chill in the fridge for 30 mins to firm up.

  2. Preheat the oven to 340℉ (170℃), using upper and lower heat.

  3. Once firmed, take a piece of dough, form it into a ball, and press it evenly into a 2.5-inch (6.5 cm) tart mold. Cut the excess dough with a knife. Prick the base with a fork. Repeat until you get 9 tart shells in total, and place them in the freezer for 10 mins.

  4. Place the tart shells in a baking tray and bake them in a preheated oven on the lowest rack, for 20-25 mins or until they're golden brown. Remove from the oven and leave for them to cool completely. Once cooled, flip and tap the bottom to release the shells from the molds. Gently squeezing the molds in the opposite sides also helps. Set aside.

Filling

  1. Add chopped chocolate into a container. Add heavy cream into a pot and heat until bubbles are forming on the edges. Pour it into the chocolate container, making sure the chocolate is all submerged. Leave it for 3 mins. Then using a hand whisk, mix everything until you get a homogenous chocolate ganache.

  2. Pour and divide the chocolate ganache evenly into the tart shells. Wiggle them a bit to smoothen the tops. Place on a tray and chill in the fridge for 30 mins before serving. Sprinkle with some coarse sea salt.

Enjoy your delicious chocolate tart!
